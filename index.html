The Consolidator

Consolidator Tool for Popular Medical Education Software. Visit drmapleleaf.github.io/consolidator

Welcome to the Consolidator

PURPOSE

This bookmarklet allows you to open multiple to-dos/evaluations at the same time. This approach saves time, bandwidth and mouse clicks for the user.

WHAT THIS IS NOT

This bookmarklet DOES NOT automate the filling and submission of evaluations. This bookmarklet does not pre-fill any responses to evaluations. To suggest otherwise is a gross misrepresentation of the capabilities of this bookmarklet.

INSTALL THE BOOKMARKLET

Drag this link:
The Consolidator
onto your favourites bar. If that doesn't work, right-click and add to favourites.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Log into your variant of the Popular Medical Education Software.
  2. Ensure that you are on http://path-to-your-Popular-Medical-Education-Software/one.php
  3. Make sure you are on the page that lists your to-dos.
  4. Make sure you have >0 to-dos.
  5. Click the bookmarklet.
  6. Abide by the terms of use.
  7. Acknowledge that this bookmarklet WILL NOT automate filling of your evaluations.
  8. Follow the rest of the instructions

COMPATIBILITY

Tested to work on Firefox & Chrome on Windows 10. YMMV on other browser/OS combinations. Can get unstable if >10 evaluations opened simulatenously.

Don't expect any help or support in using this bookmarklet. If you are having trouble "installing" it, it is probably best that you not use this bookmarklet.

UPDATES
